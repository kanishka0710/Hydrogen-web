Las Lomas

Hydrogen RC Car Team

Our Story

We are a group of students with a passion for racing and cars. Hydrogen is a developing form of fuel that has a by-product of water. We hope to gain useful knowledge on how to create hydrogen cars for the future to help alleviate our climate crisis. We currently race in the Horizon Grand Prix. It is a worldwide racing competition challenging students to make the most efficient hydrogen fuel cell R/C car. The race is an endurance race ranging from 3-6 hours depending on the stage of the competition. Our goal is to innovate and find new ways to save energy within the car and win the 2020 World Finals!
